About

I, Teraman, the creator of Terabyte Tweaker, believe that every person should have a decent computer. For many years of my life, i've had a trash computer. I know how it feels to be bullied for it, and i don't want anybody else to feel the way i felt. Then here am i, changing the world and making a better place for all gamers of all parts of the world.

This app was made by a one single person, which was 13 years old when he started to make it. He never gave up on his dream in making people have better computers. And if you have a dream of making the world a better place, do it as soon as possible, just like me.
